Our Story

From a single cart to a neighborhood ritual.

Meridian started in 2014 as a stubborn little espresso cart and a belief that great coffee shouldn't be complicated — just honest. A decade later, we roast every batch ourselves, just steps from where we serve it.

We build direct relationships with growers, pay above fair-trade rates, and roast in small batches so nothing ever sits on a shelf too long. The result is a cup that tastes like somewhere — not just like "coffee."
